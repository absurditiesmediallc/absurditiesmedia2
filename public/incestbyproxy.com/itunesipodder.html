How to subscribe to an RSS feed with iTunes

1.Download and install iTunes
2.Open iTunes
3.Click "Advanced" -> "Subscribe to Podcast"



4.A dialog will appear, enter the URL of the feed in this dialog.



5.Click "OK"

How to subscribe to an RSS feed with iPodder

1.Download and install iPodder
2.Open iPodder
3.Click on "Tools" -> "Add a Feed"



4.A dialog will appear, enter the URL of the feed into the box marked "URL"



5.Click "Save"
